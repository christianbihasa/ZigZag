ZigZag Arcade

This project is built as a respectful recreation and arcade tribute to the iconic mobile game ZigZag originally developed by Ketchapp. All conceptual gameplay rights belong to their original creators.

🎮 Controls

  • PC Desktop: Press Spacebar or click the mouse to turn.
  • Mobile Device: Tap anywhere on the screen to turn.

💡 Things to Know

  • The ball moves progressively faster the higher your score climbs.
  • We've engineered an expanded starting platform to give you a moment to focus before the path narrows.
  • The collision engine is forgiving: the ball won't trigger a Game Over until it completely clears the platform edge.
